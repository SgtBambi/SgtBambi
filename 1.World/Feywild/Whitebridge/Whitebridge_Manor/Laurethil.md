# Laurethil, Lord of Dreams

*Medium Fey (Eladrin), Chaotic Evil*

---

**Armor Class:** 17 (Natural + Fey Armor)  
**Hit Points:** 225 (18d10 + 126)  
**Speed:** 30 ft., Fly 30 ft. (hover)  

| STR | DEX | CON | INT | WIS | CHA |
|-----|-----|-----|-----|-----|-----|
| 14 (+2) | 16 (+3) | 22 (+6) | 20 (+5) | 16 (+3) | 20 (+5) |

**Saving Throws:** Dex +8, Con +11, Wis +8, Cha +10  
**Skills:** Arcana +10, Deception +10, Insight +8, Perception +8  
**Damage Resistances:** Cold; Bludgeoning, Piercing, and Slashing from Nonmagical Attacks  
**Damage Immunities:** Psychic  
**Condition Immunities:** Charmed, Frightened  
**Senses:** Truesight 60 ft., Passive Perception 18  
**Languages:** Common, Elvish, Sylvan  
**Challenge:** 12 (8,400 XP)  

---

### Traits

**Fey Presence.** (Recharge 5–6)  
Laurethil can cause each creature of his choice within 30 ft. to make a DC 17 Wisdom save or be **frightened or charmed** for 1 minute.  

**Legendary Resistance (3/Day).**  
If Laurethil fails a saving throw, he can choose to succeed instead.  

**Master of Dreams.**  
Laurethil can cast *Dream* at will without expending a spell slot, and any targets in his dreamplane cannot awaken except by *Dispel Magic* (DC 18).  

**Tree Bond.**  
While within 20 ft. of the Grasping Yew:  
- Laurethil regains 10 HP at the start of his turn if a vine is restraining a target.  
- He gains +2 AC.  
- Life Conduit: when a creature is restrained by the Grasping Yew, he can siphon 2d8 necrotic damage to heal himself as a bonus action.  

**Spellcasting.**  
Laurethil is a 10th-level spellcaster (Charisma). Spell save DC 18, +10 to hit with spell attacks.  
**Prepared Spells (at will / 3/day each as noted):**  
- *At Will:* *Mage Hand, Minor Illusion, Message*  
- *3/day:* *Fear, Hold Person, Hypnotic Pattern, Counterspell, Dispel Magic*  
- *2/day:* *Phantasmal Killer, Plane Shift (self only, to enter dreamplane)*  

---

### Actions

**Multiattack.**  
Laurethil makes two attacks: one **Fey Dagger** and one **Dream Lash**.  

**Fey Dagger.** *Melee Weapon Attack:* +9 to hit, reach 5 ft., one target. *Hit:* 8 (1d8 + 4) piercing + 7 (2d6) psychic damage.  

**Dream Lash.** *Melee Spell Attack:* +10 to hit, reach 15 ft., one target. *Hit:* 14 (3d6 + 4) psychic damage; DC 17 Wisdom save or **paralyzed until the start of his next turn**.  

---

### Legendary Actions (3 per round)  
Laurethil can take 3 legendary actions, using only one at a time at the end of another creature’s turn.  

- **Fey Dagger.** Makes one Fey Dagger attack.  
- **Vine Lash.** Deals 9 (2d8) bludgeoning damage to one creature within 10 ft. and restrains them (Strength DC 17 to escape).  
- **Dream Pulse (Costs 2 Actions).** All creatures within 20 ft. must make a DC 17 Wisdom save or take 10 (3d6) psychic damage and have disadvantage on saving throws until the start of Laurethil’s next turn.  

---

### Lair Actions (Grasping Yew)

On initiative count 20 (losing initiative ties), Laurethil can take a lair action.  

1. **Vine Grasp.** One creature within 30 ft. must succeed on DC 17 Strength save or be grappled/restrained by the Yew’s vines.  
2. **Life Drain Pulse.** Grappled creatures take 10 (3d6) necrotic damage; Laurethil regains the same amount.  
3. **Nightmare Ripple.** All creatures within 30 ft. must succeed on DC 17 Wisdom save or take 10 (3d6) psychic damage and have disadvantage on saving throws until the start of Laurethil’s next turn.  

---

### Reactions

**Vine Lash.**  
When a creature within 10 ft. of the Grasping Yew hits Laurethil, he may lash a vine to deal 9 (2d8) bludgeoning damage and restrain the target (Strength DC 17).

### Equipment / Loot

- **Ring of Winter Court (Fey, very rare):** Advantage on saving throws vs. charm/fright effects.  
- **Fey Dagger:** +1 magical, deals psychic damage.  
- **Alchemical reagents / dream-manipulation materials** (flavor / crafting hooks).
