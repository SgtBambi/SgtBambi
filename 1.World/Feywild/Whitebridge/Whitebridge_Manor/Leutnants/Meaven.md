## Maeven — Sadistic Fey Duelist
*Medium Fey (Eladrin), Lawful Evil — CR 5*

**AC:** 16 (Faewrought leathers)  
**HP:** 105 (14d8+42)  
**Speed:** 40 ft.

**STR** 12 (+1) | **DEX** 20 (+5) | **CON** 16 (+3) | **INT** 12 (+1) | **WIS** 14 (+2) | **CHA** 16 (+3)

**Saves:** Dex +9, Wis +6  
**Skills:** Acrobatics +9, Intimidation +7, Perception +6  
**Resistances:** Psychic; charm; nonmagical physical  
**Senses:** Darkvision 60 ft.  
**Languages:** Elvish, Sylvan, Common

### Traits
- **Cruel Opportunist.** Deals +2d6 psychic to frightened, charmed, or restrained targets.  
- **Fey Step (Recharge 4–6).** Teleport 30 ft. Next attack deals +10 psychic.  
- **Gleaming Reflexes.** Advantage on Dex saves vs effects she can see.

### Actions
**Multiattack.** Two attacks.  
**Fey Duellist Blade (+1 Shortsword).** *+9 to hit, 5 ft., 1d6+6 piercing + 1d6 psychic.*

**Needle of Nightmares (Recharge 5–6).**  
Ranged Spell Attack: +7 to hit, 60 ft., 3d10 psychic; DC 15 Wis save or frightened 1 minute.

### Bonus Actions
**Cruel Feint.** One creature within 30 ft makes a DC 15 Wis save or Maeven gains advantage against it.

### Equipment
- +1 shortsword of shimmering feysteel  
- Enchanted leather armor  
- Potion of Poison Resistance
