## Syrith — Shadowcourt Spy
*Medium Fey, Neutral Evil — CR 5*

**AC:** 17 (Cloak of Elvenkind, agile defense)  
**HP:** 82 (12d8+24)  
**Speed:** 30 ft., climb 20 ft.

**STR** 10 | **DEX** 20 (+5) | **CON** 14 (+2) | **INT** 14 (+2) | **WIS** 12 (+1) | **CHA** 16 (+3)

**Skills:** Stealth +12 (+16 in dim light), Deception +7, Perception +5  
**Resistances:** Poison, Psychic  
**Senses:** Darkvision 60 ft.

### Traits
- **Shadow Blend.** Invisible in dim light unless attacking.  
- **Assassin’s Precision.** First attack on an unaware creature deals +3d6 damage.  
- **Ghost Step.** No opportunity attacks when she moves out of a creature’s reach.

### Actions
**Multiattack.** Two dagger attacks.  
**Darkthorn Dagger.**  
*+9 to hit, 1d4+5 piercing + 2d6 poison.*

**Shadow Needle (Recharge 4–6).**  
Ranged: +9 to hit, 60 ft., 3d8 psychic; DC 15 Con save or **poisoned** for 1 round.

### Bonus Actions
**Vanish.** Turns invisible until start of next turn.

### Equipment
- Cloak of Elvenkind  
- Darkthorn dagger (fey-wrought)  
- Vial of paralytic poison
