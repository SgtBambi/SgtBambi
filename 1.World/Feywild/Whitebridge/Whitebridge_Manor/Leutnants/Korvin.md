## Korvin — Redcap Master-At-Arms
*Medium Fey, Chaotic Evil — CR 6*

**AC:** 18 (heavy feyplate)  
**HP:** 120 (16d8+48)  
**Speed:** 30 ft.

**STR** 20 (+5) | **DEX** 14 (+2) | **CON** 16 (+3) | **INT** 10 | **WIS** 14 (+2) | **CHA** 10

**Saves:** Str +8, Con +6  
**Skills:** Athletics +9, Intimidation +4  
**Resistances:** Nonmagical physical  
**Languages:** Sylvan, Common

### Traits
- **Redcap Savagery.** +2d6 extra damage once per turn.  
- **Brutal Command.** Bonus action: an ally deals +5 damage on its next attack.  
- **Iron Discipline.** Advantage on saves against being frightened.

### Actions
**Multiattack.** Two attacks.  
**Warhammer +1.**  
*+9 to hit, 1d8+6 bludgeoning + 2d6 extra (Savagery).*

**Crushing Blow (Recharge 5–6).**  
+3d10 damage; DC 16 Con save or **stunned**.

### Bonus Actions
**Menacing Shout.** Enemies within 10 ft. make DC 15 Wis save or have disadvantage on attacks for 1 round.

### Equipment
- Warhammer +1 (redcap-forged iron)  
- Heavy feyplate armor  
- Redcap officer’s badge
