## Veylan — Dark Fey Ritualist
*Medium Fey, Neutral Evil — CR 5*

**AC:** 15 (ritual robes + minor wards)  
**HP:** 88 (13d8+26)  
**Speed:** 30 ft.

**STR** 8 | **DEX** 14 (+2) | **CON** 14 (+2) | **INT** 18 (+4) | **WIS** 16 (+3) | **CHA** 14 (+2)

**Saves:** Int +8, Wis +7  
**Skills:** Arcana +8, Insight +7, Deception +6  
**Resistances:** Psychic; advantage vs charm  
**Senses:** Darkvision 60 ft.

### Traits
- **Ritual Wards.** Once per turn, reduce incoming spell damage by 5.  
- **Slip Away.** When dropping below half HP, teleport 60 ft. as a reaction.  
- **Dark Scholar.** Can identify spells or magical effects as a bonus action.

### Actions
**Dark Bolt.**  
Ranged Spell Attack: +8 to hit, 120 ft., 2d10 necrotic.

**Fey Binding (Recharge 5–6).**  
One target within 60 ft. must succeed on DC 16 Wis save or become **restrained**.

**Unseelie Pulse (Recharge 6).**  
15-ft radius psychic burst: 3d10 psychic, DC 16 Wis save halves.

### Bonus Actions
**Command of Shadows.**  
One ally moves 10 ft. and makes a weapon attack.

### Equipment
- Ritual knife (nonmagical)  
- Arcane component pouch  
- 1st-level spell scroll (fey-script)  
- Black-leaf grimoire (focus)
